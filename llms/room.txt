# TRTC Room

> Room management SDK providing room creation, destruction,
> member management, and room listing capabilities.

## Core Capabilities

- **Room Lifecycle**: Create, destroy, enter, exit rooms
- **Member Management**: Member list, role assignment, kick/ban users
- **Room List**: Query available rooms, filtering, pagination
