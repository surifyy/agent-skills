# TRTC Live

> Interactive live streaming SDK supporting host broadcasting, audience viewing,
> co-hosting, gifts, barrage chat, and beauty effects.

## Core Capabilities

- **Streaming**: Host broadcast, audience watch, push/pull streams
- **Interaction**: Co-hosting (mic connect), cross-room PK, audience management
- **Commerce**: Gift system, barrage messages, live room list
- **Effects**: Beauty filters, audio effects
